# Donor-first measurement plan

## Primary outcomes

1. **Qualified donor inquiry completion** — completed contact submissions with partnership intent divided by donor-hub visitors. This measures whether the donor experience produces actionable conversations.
2. **Verified donation completion rate** — provider-verified donations divided by initialized checkout sessions. Provider verification, not the browser redirect, is the source of truth.
3. **Due-diligence progression** — visitors who move from the donor hub into due-diligence, financial-accountability, reports, projects, or contact pathways divided by donor-hub visitors.

## Drivers

- donor hub → due-diligence click-through;
- donor hub → contact click-through;
- donation form validation success;
- checkout initialization success by gateway;
- report and project evidence engagement;
- newsletter acceptance for donor-oriented updates.

## Guardrails

- contact and checkout error rate;
- payment verification mismatch rate;
- Core Web Vitals: LCP, INP, and CLS;
- safeguarding-form confidentiality: no message content or personal details in analytics;
- CMS/publication-control violations: no unapproved story, media, partner, or impact record displayed.

Targets should be set only after four to eight weeks of clean baseline data. Review weekly during launch, then monthly with donor relations, communications, programmes, MEAL, and finance.
